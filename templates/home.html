About Us

WHY COMBAT SPORTS?

FITNESS, RESPECT, DISCIPLINE, HUMILITY

Embracing the art of combat as a sport, promises a physically fit lifestyle with the intrinsic values of respect, discipline, humility, and the confidence to face any and all obstacles in life. Learning how to defend yourself, training with aggresion, not anger, and using that part of your life as a therapeutic relief, builds character. At the I.C.U, we teach children and adults of all ages and genders, the value of acquiring the skill of mixed martial arts, to use it if necessary in life, but gain the pleasure of sport and fitness from it.

PHYSICAL AND MENTAL WELL-BEING

Physical activity that engages the entire body in natural and functional movements is directly related to mental and physical wellbeing, while it enhances the body's "happy" hormones. Exercising the body in it's natural state promotes longevity, inner peace, and a spiritual balance which can substantially improve the quality of one's life.

INTER-PERSONAL DEVELOPMENT

At the I.C.U we encourage familial bonds, camaraderie, and an environment of learning amidst like minded individuals from various walks of life. We have adopted the principles of engaging in sport, detached from pride, for the development of life long community ties that everyone can benefit from.
